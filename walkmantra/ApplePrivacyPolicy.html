WalkMantra: Privacy policy

Welcome to the WalkMantra app for iOS!

This is an iOS app developed by Datta Yoga Center USA (DYC USA).

We take your privacy seriously.

Data collected by the app

To the best of our knowledge and belief, we only collect First Name, Last Name, Email address, Age, Gender, Language preference, and Country at the time of registration, and we are storing the walk duration that the user is submitting. Information is kept permanently for the sole purpose of providing the service. No analytics software is present in the app.

The App uses the login credentials for authentication, which might store information in log files (for debugging purposes only). No other data is collected or tracked, to the best of our knowledge and belief.
